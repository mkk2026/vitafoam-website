## About Vitafoam Brookfields

<img src="app/public/images/storefront.jpg" alt="Vitafoam Brookfields Storefront — Brookfields, Freetown" width="100%" />

**Vitafoam Brookfields** is a leading home comfort retailer located at **29B King Harman Road, Brookfields, Freetown, Sierra Leone**. We are an authorized Vitafoam dealer and the proud home of **Salonetiti** — our own line of locally curated products made for Sierra Leonean homes.

Since 2015, we've been helping families across Sierra Leone sleep better and live more comfortably. From orthopaedic mattresses to bedding sets, pillows, cushions, and everyday home essentials — we bring quality products at fair prices, with a personal touch.
